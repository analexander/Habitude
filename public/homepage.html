What is habitude?

"How we spend our days, is of course how we spend our lives." - Annie Dillard. But are we really aware of how we are spending our days? Habitude helps you take stock of what you're doing and where you're going. Our members page gives you visual feedback of how you are spending your time, as well has how you aspire to spend it.

How does it work?

You choose where you want to go, we'll help you get there. When you login, you choose the goals that you want to improve upon. Now, you're on your way! As soon as you hit "submit" you'll be taken to your personal page. Here is where you can update what you did that day, as well as where you can see all the steps you've been taking toward a better lifestyle.
